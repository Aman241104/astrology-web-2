# Visual Identity: Islamic Love Problem Solution Expert

This visual guide defines the aesthetic for a high-conversion website that builds trust and conveys spiritual authority to a Muslim audience. It emphasizes purity (Halal) and spiritual expertise.

## 1. Primary Color Palette
- **Deep Emerald Green (#006400):** Symbolizes Islam, peace, and spiritual growth. Use for primary backgrounds and headers.
- **Gold/Metallic Gold (#D4AF37):** Symbolizes premium quality, success, and divine guidance. Use for borders, icons, and highlighted text.
- **Pure White (#FFFFFF):** Represents purity and clarity. Use for background sections to maintain a clean, professional look.
- **Midnight Blue (#191970):** Optional secondary background to convey the "mystique" of celestial science without using black magic visuals.

## 2. Typography
- **Headings:** Bold Serif font (e.g., Playfair Display or Cinzel) to convey authority, tradition, and timelessness.
- **Body Text:** Clean Sans-serif (e.g., Poppins or Montserrat) for high readability, especially on mobile.
- **Call-to-Action Text:** Bold Sans-serif with tight letter-spacing to create a sense of urgency.
- **Script (Optional):** Elegant Urdu/Arabic script for decorative purposes (e.g., "Bismillah" in the header).

## 3. Imagery & Icons
- **Key Visuals:**
    - High-quality, professional portraits of the "Hazrat" in traditional attire (Thobe, Kurta, Turban) with a warm, welcoming expression.
    - Imagery of Islamic architecture (Crescent, minarets, mosques) as subtle backgrounds or motifs.
    - Emotional stock photos: A couple smiling together, hands in prayer (Dua), or a serene sunrise.
- **Iconography:**
    - Custom gold-outlined icons for services (Crescent moon for Dua, Rings for Marriage, Open Hands for Prayer).
    - Floating/pulsing WhatsApp (Green) and Phone (Yellow) icons for immediate recognition.

## 4. UI/UX Elements
- **CTA Buttons:** 
    - Rounded corners (soft, approachable).
    - Subtle "glow" or "pulse" effect on the primary "WhatsApp" button to draw the eye.
    - Use high-contrast colors (e.g., Bright Green for WhatsApp, Gold for Call).
- **Service Cards:** 
    - Subtle drop shadows for depth.
    - Gold border for the "Featured" or most requested service.
- **Section Dividers:** 
    - Use Islamic patterns (Geometric Arabesque) as subtle dividers between sections.

## 5. Visual Consistency (No-Go Areas)
- **Avoid:** Dark/Gothic imagery, black magic symbols (voodoo dolls, dark candles, skulls).
- **Avoid:** Overly "Generic Western Astrology" symbols like tarot cards or zodiac signs (unless they are styled as celestial science/Ilm-e-Najoom).
- **Focus:** Warmth, light, spiritual purity, and a professional "consultant" look.

## 6. Mobile Experience (Primary Focus)
- **Sticky Footer:** Bottom-anchored bar with two distinct buttons: [Call Now] (left) and [WhatsApp Chat] (right).
- **Fast Loading:** Minimal heavy animations to ensure the site loads quickly in low-bandwidth areas.
- **Thumb-Friendly:** All primary CTAs should be large and easily clickable with one hand.
