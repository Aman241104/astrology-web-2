# Website Structure: Islamic Love Problem Solution Expert

This structure is designed for high conversion, targeting a Muslim audience seeking spiritual solutions (Dua/Wazifa) for relationship and life issues. It strictly avoids "Black Magic" terminology and generic "Western Astrology."

## 1. Header (Sticky)
- **Logo/Brand Name:** E.g., "Hazrat [Name] - Islamic Spiritual Healer"
- **Contact Info (Top Right):** "Call Now: +91-XXXXXXXXXX" (Phone icon)
- **WhatsApp Button:** Small "Chat with Us" button (Green)
- **Navigation:** Home, About, Services, Success Stories, Contact.

## 2. Hero Section
- **Headline:** Powerful, result-oriented (e.g., "Get Your True Love Back with 100% Halal Islamic Remedies")
- **Sub-headline:** Emphasize speed and privacy (e.g., "Solution in 24 Hours. Guaranteed Results. 100% Privacy Maintained.")
- **Primary CTAs:**
    - [Call Now] (Urgent, High Contrast)
    - [WhatsApp Expert] (Direct link to chat)
- **Visual:** Background image of the Expert (Maulana/Hazrat) in traditional attire or a serene Islamic motif.

## 3. Services Section (Directly After Hero)
*Constraint: Positioned immediately after the hero to address user pain points instantly.*
- **Layout:** 3x2 or 2x3 Grid of Cards.
- **Services Included:**
    - Dua for Love Marriage
    - Wazifa to Get Ex Back
    - Husband-Wife Dispute Solution
    - Convince Parents for Love Marriage
    - Marriage Obstacles Specialist
    - Prayer for Family Peace
- **Card Design:** Icon, Short Description, [Consult on WhatsApp] button on each card.

## 4. About the Expert (Bio)
- **Title:** "About Maulana [Name]"
- **Content:** Highlight 20+ years of experience, family lineage in spiritual healing, and global success.
- **Trust Factor:** Mention "Ilm-e-Najoom" (Islamic Astrology) and "Rohani Ilaj" (Spiritual Healing).

## 5. Trust & Success Counters (Social Proof)
- **Stats Bar:** 
    - 50,000+ Happy Clients
    - 15+ Countries Served
    - 24/7 Available
- **Testimonials:** 3-4 sliding cards with real-sounding names and specific success stories (e.g., "My husband returned after 2 years...").

## 6. Detailed Problem/Solution Section
- **Sections for specific topics:** Elaborate on why certain problems happen (evil eye/nazar, planetary shifts) and how Islamic Wazifas solve them.
- **CTA Break:** A wide banner with a phone number every 2 sections.

## 7. FAQ Section
- **Questions:** 
    - "Is this Halal?" 
    - "How soon will I see results?" 
    - "Is my information private?"
- **Answers:** Focus on Sharia-compliant methods and absolute confidentiality.

## 8. Footer
- **Quick Links:** All services linked individually for SEO.
- **Contact Details:** Full address (if applicable), Phone, Email.
- **Bottom Bar:** Copyright and "Consultation is Private" disclaimer.

## 9. Persistent CTA Strategy (Crucial)
- **Mobile Floating Bar:** A sticky footer on mobile with two buttons: [Call Now] and [WhatsApp].
- **WhatsApp Bubble:** A pulsing green icon in the bottom right corner.
- **Exit Intent Popup:** "Don't Leave Without a Solution! Get a Free 5-Min Consultation." (Lead to WhatsApp).
